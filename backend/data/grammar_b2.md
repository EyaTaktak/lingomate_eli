# Grammar B2

## Mixed tenses in narrative
- I had finished my homework when he arrived.

## Third conditional and mixed conditionals
- If I had studied harder, I would have passed.

## Past modals of deduction
- He must have forgotten his keys.

## Causatives
- I had my car repaired. / I got my hair cut.

## Non-defining relative clauses
- My brother, who lives in London, is a teacher.

## Reported speech
- He said he was tired.

## Inversion and emphasis
- Never have I seen such a beautiful place.
