# Grammar C1

## Advanced conditionals and inversions
- Had I known, I would have acted differently.

## Complex noun and adjective clauses
- The book that I borrowed from the library was fascinating.

## Advanced passive constructions
- The project is being carried out by experts.

## Ellipsis and substitution
- I like pizza; she does too.

## Future perfect / Future continuous
- By next year, I will have finished my degree.
- This time tomorrow, I will be flying to Paris.

## Cleft sentences
- It was John who solved the problem.

# Vocabulary C1
- Abstract and academic concepts
- Nuanced emotional and evaluative language
- Idiomatic expressions and metaphors
- Formal writing structures
- Paraphrasing and summarizing skills
