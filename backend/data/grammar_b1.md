# Grammar B1

## Present perfect vs Past simple
- I have eaten lunch. (present perfect)
- I ate lunch yesterday. (past simple)

## First and Second Conditionals
- First: If it rains, I will stay home.
- Second: If I were rich, I would travel.

## Modals
- should, must, might, can, could

## Gerunds and Infinitives
- I enjoy reading. (gerund)
- I want to go. (infinitive)

## Relative clauses (defining)
- The man who called you is my uncle.

## Passive voice (present and past)
- The book is read by many people.
- The letter was sent yesterday.

# Vocabulary B1
- Jobs and work life
- Health and fitness
- Travel and holidays
- Education and school life
- Describing feelings and emotions
