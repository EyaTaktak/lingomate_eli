# Grammar A1

## Verb to be
- Forms: am, is, are
- Example: I am happy. He is tall. They are students.

## Present Simple
- Affirmative: I play football.
- Negative: I do not play football.
- Questions: Do you play football?

## Have got / Has got
- I have got a pen. She has got a cat.

## There is / There are
- There is a book on the table.
- There are two chairs in the room.

## Basic modal verbs (can for ability)
- I can swim. She can speak English.

## Articles
- a / an / the
- Example: a dog, an apple, the sun

## Possessive adjectives
- my, your, his, her, its, our, their
- Example: This is my book.

# Vocabulary A1
- Numbers, dates, days of the week
- Family and friends
- Everyday objects
- Food and drink
- Basic adjectives: big, small, hot, cold
