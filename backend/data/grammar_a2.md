# Grammar A2

## Past Simple
- Regular verbs: I visited London.
- Irregular verbs: She went home.

## Present Continuous
- I am studying English.
- He is playing football.

## Future with "going to"
- I am going to travel tomorrow.

## Countable and Uncountable nouns with some/any
- Some apples, any milk

## Comparatives and Superlatives
- Comparative: bigger, more interesting
- Superlative: biggest, most interesting

## Basic Conjunctions
- and, but, because

## Should for advice
- You should eat more vegetables.

# Vocabulary A2
- Shopping and money
- Daily routines and chores
- Weather and seasons
- Hobbies and interests
- Describing places and people
